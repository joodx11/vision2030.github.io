Saudi Vision 2030

❝ My primary goal is to be an exemplary and leading nation in all aspects, and I will work with you in achieving this endeavour. ❞

— Custodian of the Two Holy Mosques, King Salman Bin Abdulaziz Al-Saud

Vision 2030 Goals

Saudi Arabia's Vision 2030 outlines a strategic roadmap to transform the Kingdom into a global model of excellence. The goals are divided into social, economic, and governmental priorities, each supporting national development and long-term sustainability.

Economic Diversification

Objective: Reduce dependence on oil revenues.

  • Develop non-oil sectors such as tourism, entertainment, mining, manufacturing, and logistics.
  • Invest in renewable energy and technology.
  • Encourage private sector growth and entrepreneurship.

Empowering Citizens

Objective: Build a productive and inclusive society.

  • Increase women's participation in the workforce and leadership roles.
  • Expand training programs and scholarships for youth.
  • Promote volunteering and social responsibility.

Enhancing Quality of Life

Objective: Make Saudi Arabia one of the best places to live.

  • Improve public health services and facilities.
  • Upgrade education systems with modern technology and curricula.
  • Expand cultural, recreational, and sports programs.

Sustainable Infrastructure

Objective: Create modern, smart cities.

  • Build efficient public transport systems.
  • Develop housing programs for affordable, high-quality homes.
  • Improve urban planning and green spaces.

Environmental Protection

Objective: Safeguard natural resources for future generations.

  • Launch green energy projects (solar, wind).
  • Reduce carbon emissions and pollution.
  • Conserve water and biodiversity.

Government Efficiency & Digital Transformation

Objective: Make government more transparent, efficient, and tech-driven.

  • Digitize public services and reduce bureaucracy.
  • Measure performance with key indicators.
  • Enhance accountability and fight corruption.

National Identity and Culture

Objective: Preserve and promote Saudi cultural heritage.

  • Restore historical sites.
  • Support artists and creatives.
  • Promote Saudi culture globally.

Vision Pillars

Saudi Arabia's Vision 2030 is built upon three core pillars that shape the Kingdom's aspirations for a thriving future. Each pillar represents a strategic priority to drive transformation and ensure long-term sustainability and prosperity.

1. A Vibrant Society

We are creating a society with strong roots, rich traditions, and a thriving culture. By enhancing quality of life, promoting cultural heritage, and encouraging sports and entertainment, we aim to foster community engagement and national pride.

  • Preserving Islamic and national values
  • Promoting cultural and recreational activities
  • Ensuring healthy and fulfilling lives

2. A Thriving Economy

Empowering citizens, diversifying the economy, and investing in innovation are central to building a strong and sustainable economy. Vision 2030 opens new doors for business, entrepreneurship, and job creation.

  • Supporting local industries and SMEs
  • Attracting foreign investment
  • Advancing education and skills for the future

3. An Ambitious Nation

We strive to build a transparent, accountable, and effective government that serves its people with integrity. Through digital transformation and public sector efficiency, we are setting new standards for governance and performance.

  • Enhancing government effectiveness
  • Fostering social responsibility
  • Promoting fiscal sustainability

Vision Realization Programs

Vision Realization Programs (VRPs) are specialized initiatives launched to translate Vision 2030's goals into actionable plans. Each program addresses specific national priorities and is led by government entities.

Key Programs:

  • National Industrial Development and Logistics Program (NIDLP): Enhances Saudi Arabia's position as a global industrial and logistics hub.
  • Quality of Life Program: Improves the living standards by expanding cultural, sports, and recreational activities.
  • Fiscal Balance Program: Ensures sustainable public finances and spending efficiency.
  • Human Capability Development Program: Focuses on education reform and skills for the future.
  • Digital Government Authority (DGA): Accelerates digital transformation across the public sector.

Mega Projects

Saudi Arabia is launching unprecedented mega-projects that redefine urban living, tourism, and sustainability.

Key Projects:

  • NEOM: A $500 billion futuristic city powered by renewable energy and AI.
  • The Red Sea Project: A luxury tourism destination along Saudi's pristine western coastline.
  • Qiddiya: The Kingdom's capital of entertainment, sports, and culture.
  • Diriyah Gate: A heritage-focused project reviving the birthplace of the Kingdom.
  • King Salman Park: The largest urban park in the world, located in Riyadh.

Education in 2030

Vision 2030 aims to transform the education sector into a globally competitive system that prepares students for future careers.

Focus Areas:

  • Revamping school curricula to emphasize innovation, critical thinking, and STEM
  • Encouraging partnerships with international universities
  • Expanding vocational and technical training
  • Increasing private sector involvement in education
  • Promoting lifelong learning and digital learning platforms

Sustainability

Environmental protection and resource sustainability are at the core of Vision 2030's long-term plans.

Key Initiatives:

  • The Saudi Green Initiative: Aims to plant 10 billion trees, reduce carbon emissions, and increase protected areas
  • Water desalination and conservation programs
  • Waste management and recycling projects
  • Sustainable urban planning
  • Circular carbon economy model implementation

Healthcare

Vision 2030 seeks to ensure comprehensive and accessible healthcare for all residents through modernization and efficiency.

Key Strategies:

  • Enhancing healthcare infrastructure and digital health services
  • Promoting preventive care and disease control
  • Encouraging private investment in healthcare
  • Introducing value-based care models
  • Expanding health insurance coverage

Digital Transformation

Digital innovation is a critical enabler of Vision 2030, driving government efficiency and economic growth.

Key Highlights:

  • Nationwide 5G rollout and fiber network expansion
  • Smart government platforms and e-services
  • AI and big data applications in public services
  • E-commerce and digital payments ecosystem
  • Support for tech startups and entrepreneurs

Key Institutions:

  • SDAIA (Saudi Data and AI Authority) – https://sdaia.gov.sa
  • Digital Government Authority – https://dga.gov.sa

Culture and Entertainment

Vision 2030 revives and celebrates Saudi Arabia's rich culture while creating a thriving entertainment industry.

Major Areas of Development:

  • Opening cinemas and theaters across the Kingdom
  • Hosting global concerts, sports events, and festivals
  • Supporting Saudi artists, musicians, and filmmakers
  • Launching cultural exchange programs
  • Establishing museums, galleries, and heritage sites

Entities Leading the Vision:

  • General Entertainment Authority – https://www.gea.gov.sa
  • Ministry of Culture – https://www.moc.gov.sa/en

Renewable Energy

Saudi Arabia is shifting from an oil-based economy to a diversified energy portfolio that includes renewables.

Key Projects:

  • Sakaka PV Solar Plant and other solar initiatives
  • Dumat Al Jandal Wind Farm
  • National Renewable Energy Program (NREP)
  • Hydrogen fuel and green energy research
  • Targeting 50% renewable energy in power generation by 2030

Leading Authority:

Ministry of Energy – https://www.moenergy.gov.sa
